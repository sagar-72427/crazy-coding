Plant & infrastructure

Narayani Rice Mill manufacturing and processing facility is ideally located at Bhramar pur,Berham pur(Ganjam), equipped with the modern technology, constantly upgrading the rice processing & control systems, at par with the global standards for optimization of the facilities.Our products are considered supreme in quality, texture, uniformity & cleanliness. The strategic location of plant ensures expeditious transportation of harvested paddy from specially identified collection centers across paddy growing small villages etc, thus minimizing transit damage to the delicate grain of rice.
